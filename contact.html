Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long is each coaching session?

+

Each session runs 60-90 minutes. The initial discovery session may go up to 2 hours as we map out your unique journey and goals.

Are sessions online or in-person?

+

Both! Online sessions via Zoom are available worldwide, and in-person sessions can be arranged based on location. Online sessions are just as effective.

Is the first session really free?

+

Absolutely! The 30-minute discovery call is complimentary. It's an opportunity for us to understand your goals and see if we're the right fit. No commitment required.

How many sessions do I need?

+

Most programs run 8-12 sessions over 2-3 months. However, every journey is unique. Some clients see breakthroughs in 4-6 sessions, while others prefer ongoing support. We'll customize a plan for you.

What's your cancellation policy?

+

We understand life happens! You can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your session at no charge. We're flexible and understanding.

Do you offer couples sessions?

+

Yes! Our Relationship Coaching program has dedicated tracks for both individuals and couples. Both partners attend sessions together, with optional individual sessions as needed.
