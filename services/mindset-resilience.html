10-Module Curriculum

What You'll Master

01

Fixed vs Growth Mapping

Map your current mindset patterns and identify where fixed thinking is holding you back.

02

Failure Reframing

Transform your relationship with failure. Learn to see every setback as data for growth.

03

Self-Talk Awareness

Become aware of your inner dialogue and learn to redirect negative self-talk into empowering narratives.

04

Emotional Trigger Mapping

Identify your emotional triggers, understand their origins, and develop healthy response patterns.

05

Effort & Learning Orientation

Shift from outcome-fixation to process-love. Celebrate effort and learning over perfection.

06

Receiving Feedback

Develop the skill of receiving feedback without defensiveness, turning critique into fuel for growth.

07

Comparison Detox

Break free from the comparison trap. Build a practice of self-referential growth and gratitude.

08

Resilience Micro Habits

Build daily micro-habits that compound into rock-solid emotional resilience over time.

09

Courage Sketch

Define what courage looks like in your life and practice bold action in small, meaningful ways.

10

Personal Mindset Charts

Create your personalized mindset growth tracker and resilience dashboard for ongoing self-monitoring.
