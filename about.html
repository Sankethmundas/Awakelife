Your Coach

Nivedita Hakkapakki

Life Coach • Transformation Guide • Speaker

Nivedita Hakkapakki is a passionate life coach dedicated to helping individuals unlock their true potential. With a unique blend of ancient wisdom and contemporary coaching methodologies, she creates deeply transformative experiences for her clients.

Her journey into coaching began from a personal quest for meaning and purpose. After experiencing her own profound transformation, Nivedita committed herself to guiding others through similar journeys of self-discovery, growth, and empowerment.

Today, she has helped over 500 individuals across the globe break free from limiting beliefs, build unshakable mindsets, align with their core values, and create lives filled with purpose and presence.
